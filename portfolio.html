Work Bytes

In this project we were asked to work as a group to come up with an original application concept based on API's of our own choosing. After a bit of pivoting due to usibility issues with a few API's that we initially chose, we landed on the idea of an application that would allow you to choose a job based on the restaurants located nearby.
