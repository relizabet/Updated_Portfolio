About Me

I am an aspiring Full Stack Web Developer. I have a passion for creating things be it through ink, clay, or code. My ultimate goal is to start my own Cyber Security firm after completing my Bachelors of Science in Computer Science. I currently work full time for Comcast as a CAE 4 handling high security accounts for our customers that require additional identity security.

In my personal life I am a new mother to a baby boy. I spend the majority of my time working on personal coding projects with my son strapped to my chest.
